Data Privacy & Protection Policy

At Jmcy Web Solutions, we value the integrity of your business data. This policy explains how JmcyFlow handles information for your company and your clients.

1. Data We Collect

To provide a functional inventory engine, we store the following data on your behalf:

  • Company Profiles: Business name, logo, and preferred color schemes.
  • Inventory Data: Product names, quantities, SKUs, and transaction history.
  • Staff Accounts: Login emails and encrypted credentials for your team members.
  • Sales Records: Transaction amounts, timestamps, and itemized lists.

2. Data Isolation (Your Vault)

JmcyFlow is built on a "Multi-Tenant" architecture. This means while all users use the same application, your data is kept in a separate digital vault. We use Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure that staff from "Company A" can never view or access data from "Company B."

Pro-Tech Security: All data is stored in a real-time cloud database (Supabase) with enterprise-grade encryption. We do not sell, rent, or share your business data with third-party advertisers.

3. Use of Information

We use your data strictly to:

  • Generate your inventory and sales reports.
  • Enable staff to process transfers and returns.
  • Maintain system security and prevent unauthorized logins.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

JmcyFlow uses local storage to keep your session active and remember your portal preferences (like your custom logo). We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

5. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. Upon termination of your managed service, you may request a full export of your records. 14 days after a service expires, all associated data is purged from our production servers to maintain security.
